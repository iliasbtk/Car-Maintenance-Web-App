VehicleSmart - Car Check

VehicleSmart is an android app.It’s more useful to check a car you want to buy as it pulls information about MOT, Road Tax, whether it’s stolen, scrapped, written off. Whereas my app is more about tracking costs and automated reminders for car maintenance and document renewal. Unlike our app, VehicleSmart does not have customized statistics and charts apart from yearly mileage from MOT history. Fuelio

Fuelio is an android app. It’s similar to my app in terms of main goal but requires more user effort to input some data such as MOT and tax information. The same applies to maintenance services where the user needs to type in the names, and fill in multiple fields. When it comes to reminders about MOT and Road tax, our app collects the data automatically from DVLA’s website and generates reminders automatically. Most of the data inputs are based on a dropdown list which requires less effort from the user and enhances the usability. In our app, we avoid typing unless it is really necessary.
